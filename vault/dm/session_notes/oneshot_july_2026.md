# Backstory 

The world we play in is a former human kingdom (MISSING NAME) which in its borderlands struggles against occasional demonic incursions. 
Through intrigues and finally a coup the reigning family was usurped by high elves, which left all but one of the members of the former monarchy dead.
The new reigning high elves strive to bring purity and to more aggressively purge the demonic incursions for which they expect large amounts of resources from all the towns, villages, and hamlets under its influence. By force, if must be.

In the kingdom's territories there is a dwarven mining town (MISSING NAME) hidden in the mountain that has access to a lot of mithril that the kingdom wants to reach to forge powerful siege weapons but can't because the trade routes were demolished on purpose once the kingdom was overthrown and the mining tunnel maps present in the castle were burnt to prevent them from falling into the hands of the new rulers.

There are often royal search parties out scouting the hills, forests, and mountains surrounding the mining town attempting to find the hidden paths that still lead there.

The high elves have set up a preliminary excavation at one of the mountain sides in an attempt to dig for mithril and other resources themselves, the early stage digging is largely achieved by submitting prisoners and former kingdom loyalists to slave labor.

There is a resistance that three of the player characters are part of, routinely robbing royal tax collection caravans and redistributing the stolen resources back among the people they were taken from. This has to be done in secret and carefully, since, if the crown believes self-serving bandits are the ones stealing the goods, there is less incentive to collectively punish commoners for it.

The player characters are:
MISSING NAME - a dwarf barbarian from the mining town.
MISSING NAME - a drow ranger from the underdark the mining town connects to.
MISSING NAME - a high elf druid who is the sister of the current usurper king, who ran away from court at a young age to live as a hermit in the mountains.
Asteria, who is a rogue changeling and a cuckoo's child in the human royal family that was overthrown, she is a princess and the last living heir to the throne. 
MISSING NAME - A Dragon sorceress in human form that doesn't like to transform into her dragon form, she is the treasurer of the crown since before the overthrow, so she knows Asteria since she was a child, and was spared and stayed in her position (albeit noticeably less and less trusted with important tasks and decisions by the elven aristocracy). She seeks to disenchant her dragon form (to no longer be a dragon).

Backstory elements: Asteria who until then was still living shapeshifted in the castle contacted the rebellion about a valuable royal caravan to attack. 
The caravan had the royal treasurer (PC) on a mission to ascertain the viablity, options and potentials ways to finance the mithril drilling project.
Asteria was travelling along with her, hiding in plain sight impersonating staff. 
The resistance, including the PCs, ambushes her caravan and to make her follow without violent resistance Asteria reveals her true self to the treasurer. 
Asteria joins the resistance outside of the castle, hoping to find allegiance in the resistance for her claim to the throne and to overthrow the elves.
The treasurer learns that the druid PC in the resistance knows of a powerful enchantress down in the underdark that can potentially undo her dragon form. 
The resistance promises to show the treasurer to the enchantress if she would help them. Asteria also promises the treasurer, who was dear to her family, that she would retain her position at court if Asteria was to claim back the throne.

We find ourselves in a hamlet near where the mountain excavations are beginning..
